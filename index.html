About me

I am a digital curator and multidisciplinary designer based in Stockholm. My work focuses on the intersection of technical precision and human-centric editorial design. I believe that every pixel should serve a purpose, and every interaction should tell a story without being loud.

With over eight years of experience, I've collaborated with global brands and independent studios to craft digital experiences that transcend the typical browser experience, transforming interfaces into digital galleries.
